Our Mission

we revolutionize plastic recycling by providing accessible
pickup services for plastic scrap, empowering communities to
contribute to a cleaner planet. Through innovative processing,
we transform collected plastic into sustainable products such
as public charging points, combustible pallets, and more,
fostering an eco-conscious circular economy that benefits
both people and the environment.

Our Vision

To lead the way in sustainable recycling,
transforming plastic waste into valuable products that
serve communities and the environment alike.
We envision a world where waste is repurposed into solutions
that power everyday life, reduce environmental impact,
and inspire a global commitment to responsible resource use
and sustainable innovation

What We Are Doing

We collect plastic scrap directly from local communities
and recycle it into practical, sustainable products.
Our key initiatives include creating public charging stations
with advertising spaces and developing a range of recycled plastic
products available for purchase on our website. By working closely
with the community, we turn plastic waste into valuable resources
that support local needs, reduce environmental impact,
and strengthen community connections

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Team 404 started as a small venture with big dreams. With a team of dedicated Students who strive to bring the best solutions Four our future. Our journey has been one of growth, learning, and continuous improvement.

Our Values

Sustainability - We are committed to reducing plastic waste and promoting sustainable practices Community Engagement - Our roots are in the community. By involving local residents in recycling efforts Innovation - We continuously seek creative ways to repurpose plastic waste into practical, impactful products, bringing modern solutions like charging stations and recyclable goods to everyday life. Accessibility - We strive to make recycling easy and beneficial for all. Responsibility - We take pride in our role as environmental stewards,
committed to creating a positive impact.
